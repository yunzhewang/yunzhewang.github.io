Education & Experience

About me

I'm now a lecturer at Suzhou University of Science and Technology. My research interests cover data mining, dynamic graph analysis and information visualization. I achieved a Bachelor's degree from SooChow University in 2013, a Master's degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2014 and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2020.
