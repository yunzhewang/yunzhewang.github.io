About me

I'm now a lecturer at Suzhou University of Science and Technology. My research interests cover data mining, dynamic graph analysis and information visualization. I achieved a Bachelor's degree from SooChow University in 2013, a Master's degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2014 and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2020.

2015 ~ 2020 Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Ph.D. candidate
2014 ~ 2015 Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University Research Assistant
2013 ~ 2014 Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong Master of Science
2012 ~ 2013 Faculty of Mathematics, The University of Waterloo Exchange
2009 ~ 2013 School of Computer Science and Technology, Soochow University Bachelor
